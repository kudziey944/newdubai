Who We Are

A Platform Built for Zimbabwe's Ambition

New Dubai Development Group (Pvt) Ltd is a Zimbabwean exhibition and development company committed to building world-class platforms that bring industry, government, and investment together under one roof.

We don't just organise events — we build permanent infrastructure for Zimbabwe's economic future. Our flagship initiative, the IRECA Hub, is a year-round showcase environment based at the Cold Storage Company Complex in Kadoma, Mashonaland West.

◆
Vision

To position Zimbabwe as Africa's premier hub for renewable energy, agricultural innovation, and climate-smart industrial investment.

◆
Mission

To create permanent, world-class exhibition infrastructure that catalyses Foreign Direct Investment and drives Zimbabwe's National Development Strategy 2 goals.

◆
What Drives Us

The belief that Zimbabwe's greatest assets — its land, its energy, its people — deserve a global stage. We build that stage.

🏛️

What We Do

Four Pillars Driving Zimbabwe Forward

Every exhibition we host is built around four interconnected sectors that define Zimbabwe's economic future.

🌱

Climate-Smart Agriculture & Precision Farming

Permanent model farm environments and learning centres showcasing Agricultural AI technology using cutting-edge European and Asian operational standards.

☀️

Renewable Energy Integration

Live showcases for commercial solarization, advanced hybrid micro-grids, and decentralised bio-energy structures supporting full rural industrialisation.

⛏️

Sustainable Mining Partnerships

A unique corporate engagement interface connecting mining conglomerates with environmental engineers for clean extraction and soil rehabilitation.

🌍

Climate Change Mitigation Infrastructure

Permanent pavilions highlighting climate resilience models, ecological restoration methodologies, and green construction techniques.

Why Attend IRECA Hub?

Presidential Solarisation Access

Registration qualifies participants for the Presidential Solarisation Scheme Phase 2, including solar borehole systems and water pumping kits.

Borehole-Solar Kits

Communities and institutions will be assessed for solar solutions and linked to approved suppliers and financiers.

Live Demonstrations

See working solar systems in real time. Interact with engineers and technology providers.

Networking & Investment

Engage government decision-makers, meet financiers and DFIs, form partnerships with EPCs and manufacturers.

Policy Intelligence

Understand Zimbabwe's national renewable energy policy direction and gain insights into upcoming projects.

ICT & Connectivity

Solar-powered ICT hubs, small computer labs, and pilot EV charging stations.
